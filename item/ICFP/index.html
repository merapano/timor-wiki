Baucau College - Instituto Católico para a Formação de Professores (ICFP)

In 2002, ACU was invited by the Marist Brothers to join the team helping to rebuild Timor-Leste educational infrastructure In Baucau, the 2nd largest city in Timor-Leste.

ACU, through IACE, is collaborating with Instituto Católico para Formação de Professores (ICFP), Baucau, East Timor, on the project, "East Timor Capacity Building: Sustained International Collaboration"

The project is focused on developing Timorese staff and students, institutional infrastructure and sustainability.

By the end of 2013, 325 teachers have graduated from ICFP with a Bachelor of Teaching in primary education and eight staff are studying for, or have completed, a Master in Education degree.
