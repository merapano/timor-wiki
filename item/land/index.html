オーストロネジアン

オーストロネジアン一般の「土地」については [@reuter-land] に詳しい。

People’s rights to land or other localised resources and their social identity are thus inseparable, and both are 11 defined by reference to time, or ‘precedence’. Status and rights to land are indexed on their relative proximity to the sacred origin place or ‘source’ of this path, so that the ultimate ritual overlordship of a territory is often vested in the human representative of this source. (pp 11--12)
