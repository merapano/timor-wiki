Nationalism in Timor Leste

[[!toc levels=2]]

This page is about the sympoisum to be held in Osaka in November 2017 on the Timor Leste nationalism.

The project is funded by JSPS (no. 25300046).

The title is:

Nationalism in Timor Leste: Seen from Anthropological View Points

Date and place

  • Date: 12 November 2017
  • Time: from 9:30am to 6pm
  • Place: Osaka University Nakanoshima Center
  • Room: 702
  • extra Room (501) for child care etc

Presenters

  • Satoshi Nakagawa (Osaka University)
  • Andrew McWilliam (Western Sydney University)
  • Michael Leach (Swinburne University of Technology)
  • Shintaro Fukutake (Sophia University)
  • Toru Ueda (Setsunan University)
  • Yoshinari Morita (Osaka University)
  • Wakana Okuda (Kanda University of International Studies)
